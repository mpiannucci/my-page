URI ASV

The Ocean Engineering Department at the University of Rhode Island competes in an Autonomous Surface Vehicle competition every year. A set list of tasks is given for a robotic boat to complete completely on its own. This is my first year contributing. My role on the team has become navigation configurating with Python and embedded micro-processor development with C++. This has allowed me to refine and bring my object oriented programming skills to a real world arena.

SimpleSolver

As an engineering student, it has frustrated me that were no quadratic equation solving applications with an elegant user interface. I strived to create a simple, but useful app with a user interface that matched these characteristics. Thus, SimpleSolver for Android was born. This application is now available on Google Play for free.
