HackWinds

The easiest way to check the waves in Rhode Island

Features

  • iOS
  • Android

Live Streaming Camera

View the HD stream straight from Narragansett Beach wherever you are.

5 Day Forecast

Get the latest forecast information for the upcoming 5 days.

Animated Forecast Charts

View animated swell, wind, and period forecast charts for each of the upcoming five days.

Live Buoy Data

Get the latest NOAA buoy reports, showing the current swell in the water.

Tide Information

View the upcoming tides, as well as the sunrise and sunset for the day so you can plan whether to dawn patrol or leave work early.
