HackWinds

The easiest way to check the waves in Rhode Island


Features

  • iOS
  • Android

Live Beach View

View the HD camera images straight from Narragansett Beach wherever you are.

5 Day Forecast

Get the latest forecast information for the upcoming 7 days straight from RhodyCast.

Animated Forecast Charts

View animated wave, wind, and period forecast charts for each day of the upcoming week.

Live Buoy Data

Get the latest NOAA buoy reports, showing the current swell in the water. Convienently view the current wave spectra breakdown from Block Island, Montauk, and Nantucket.

Tide Information

View the upcoming tides, as well as the sunrise and sunset for the day so you can plan whether to dawn patrol or leave work early.
